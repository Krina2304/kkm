Hostel Facilities

About Our Hostel

Kanya Kelavani Mandal offers safe, comfortable, and well-maintained hostel accommodation for our students. Our hostel provides a supportive environment conducive to study, personal growth, and healthy living.

Facilities

  • Spacious well-ventilated rooms
  • 24x7 security and CCTV surveillance
  • Clean and hygienic bathrooms
  • Mess with nutritious meals
  • Common recreation room

Amenities

  • 24-hour water supply
  • Regular cleaning and maintenance
  • Power backup
  • Medical assistance
  • Quiet and peaceful study environment
