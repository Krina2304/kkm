Objectives

  • To promote holistic education among girls in intellectual, moral, physical, commercial, industrial, artistic, home science, and social science fields.
  • To establish and run hostels, schools, colleges, and technical institutes for girls’ education.
  • To provide safe and affordable hostel facilities from 7th standard to college level.
  • To maintain student-friendly infrastructure with essential amenities.
  • To operate a hygienic and well-equipped hostel mess.
  • To foster self-reliance, discipline, and personal growth among girl students.
  • To continue the legacy of promoting girls' education and empowerment, building on the foundation laid in 1953 with the establishment of the girls' hostel.
