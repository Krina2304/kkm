About Us

Our Mission

To promote girls’ education in academic, vocational, and social fields.
To provide safe and affordable hostel facilities from 7th standard to college.
To support all-round development through wellequipped infrastructure and nurturing care since 1953.

Our Vision

To empower women through quality and affordable education in all dimensions —intellectual, moral, physical, vocational, and social—by creating a nurturing environment that supports lifelong learning and self-reliance.

Our History

Shri Kanya Kelvani Mandal Established in 1953, is one of the oldest management in the field of women's education in gujrat.TheKelvani Mandal has established Educational institutes from pre-primary schools to Bachelor and Master degree colleges. Shri J.M. Patel arts and Smt. M.N. Patel Commerce Mahila College was started in June 1991,affilated to Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University Grants commission under 2F and 12B on 23 rd June 1999. Apart from P.G. center in Gujrat & Hindi subjects,the post graduate education in the sublect of commerce has started in June 2014. Looking to the need of the higher education, college had also started B.A.(English) programme from June 2013. The Principal was recognised as Ph.D guide in Economics in 2003. Ph.D. Guidance in Gujarati and Hindi is also available in the college. Remedial coaching classed for weak students and classes of cooking& bakery and beauty parlor for students & society are arranged regulaly. The college has got'B' grade byNAAC in March 2008 and again we are going for NAAC re-Accreditation on July 2015.
